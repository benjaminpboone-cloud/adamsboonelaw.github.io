About the Attorney

Taylor D. Boone is a partner and managing attorney at Adams Boone Law, practicing since 1999 and licensed in both Virginia and New York. With over 26 years of experience in litigation, business operations, and complex legal matters, Mr. Boone represents individuals and businesses with a practical, disciplined, and results-focused approach.

Business Law Background

Experience advising clients on contracts, employment matters, and business transactions (generally under $5 million), with practical guidance and clear communication.

Business Law Resume (Highlights)

  • Business & Litigation Experience: Advises and represents businesses in contract drafting and negotiation, employment matters, joint ventures, and business transactions, generally involving matters under $5 million.
  • Law Firm Leadership: Partner and Managing Attorney responsible for firm operations, marketing, personnel, and administration.
  • Prior Litigation Work: Represented plaintiffs and defendants in complex business litigation in Virginia and New York state and federal courts.
  • Medical & Professional Defense Background: Formerly defended physicians, hospitals, and healthcare providers in complex medical malpractice litigation through trial.
  • Academic Experience: Adjunct Professor of Business Law, instructing upper-level courses required for a Business Management degree program.
  • Education: Juris Doctor, Syracuse University College of Law; M.B.A., University of Virginia Darden School of Business.

Personal Injury Resume (Highlights)

  • Personal Injury Litigation: Represents individuals injured in a wide range of matters, including motor vehicle accidents, premises liability, medical malpractice, and serious injury cases arising from various causes.
  • Trial Experience: Extensive civil litigation experience in Virginia and New York state and federal courts, including jury and bench trials.
  • Medical Malpractice Background: Former defense counsel for physicians, hospitals, and nursing facilities, providing unique insight into complex medical and injury cases.
  • Government Service: Former U.S. Air Force Judge Advocate and Special Assistant United States Attorney, handling criminal prosecutions and federal tort claims.
  • Leadership & Management: Managed teams of attorneys, paralegals, and staff across both government and private-sector legal organizations.
  • Languages: Fluent in Spanish.

Mr. Boone is admitted to practice in Virginia and New York, as well as the United States District Court for the Eastern District of Virginia and the United States Bankruptcy Court for the Eastern District of Virginia.
