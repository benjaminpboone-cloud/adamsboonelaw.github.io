Practice Areas

Personal Injury

Adams Boone Law represents clients in all types of personal injury matters, including but not limited to:

  • Automobile, bus, and taxi accidents
  • Boating and aviation accidents
  • Medical malpractice
  • Slip and fall injuries
  • Serious injuries arising from any cause

Business Law

The firm advises and represents businesses and individuals in a wide range of business law matters, including:

  • Contract drafting, review, and negotiation
  • Employment-related matters
  • Purchase and sale of businesses
  • General business counseling for transactions under $5 million

Legal services are provided to clients throughout Virginia and New York.
